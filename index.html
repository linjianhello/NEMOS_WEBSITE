ABOUT US


Our group is in the Department of Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at Colorado School of Mines.

Much of our work focuses on resource allocation in networks, smart grid, and cloud computing, incentive mechanism design, and network security and privacy.

We are looking for self-motivated and hardworking students who are interested in tackling algorithmic, optimization, and economic challenges across different areas, including networking, crowdsourcing, smart grid, and security and privacy.
